The Process

How Zelo Vita Works

A simple, transparent, four-step process designed to get you the medications you need — without the complexity.

💊 1

Submit Your Request

Share your prescription and medication details through our secure form. Our team reviews every submission personally within hours.

🔍 2

We Source & Verify

Our specialists identify certified suppliers worldwide and verify the authenticity, quality, and regulatory status of your medication.

📋 3

Customs Clearance

We prepare and file all customs documentation, import permits, and regulatory paperwork so your package moves through without delay.

📦 4

Delivered to You

Your medication arrives securely with full tracking. Temperature-sensitive medications are handled with proper cold-chain logistics.
